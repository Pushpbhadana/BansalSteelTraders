Journey with SAIL

Steel Authority Of India Limited (SAIL) is India’s largest steel producing public sector company. Bansal Steel Traders started as a fabrication firm in 1980s by Late Sh. Mohan Lal Bansal, later it shifted its business to steel trading. Tackling huge struggles of business, Bansal Steel Traders has taken a long leap to reach to a position where they are lifting huge quantity from SAIL and have an experience of more than 40 Years in Industry.

Bansal Steel Trader is now a Channel Partner with SAIL for Structural items including NEX sections like NPB, WPB. Acknowledged by SAIL management at various occasions, BST is a honest and trustworthy firm, who understand and values customer needs and requirements.
